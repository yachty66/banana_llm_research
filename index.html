At Banana LLM research 🍌 our goal is it to provide you with an overview 📚 of the most popular LLM leaderboards that exist. We are in the process of providing a link 🔗 for each model to our playground, where you can test out the model in a fun, graphical interface 🎮.

We also provide our API 🛠️, so you can immediately get started with your script to test if the model meets your requirements. Additionally, we provide the costs 💰 for 100 tokens per second and a link to a guide 📖 that shows you how to easily set up this model on Banana 🍌 for your use.
